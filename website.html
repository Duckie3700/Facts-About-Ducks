Ducks live in all fresh water and sea water. The only place they do not live in is Antarctica.

Male ducks are called drakes, females are called hen, and babies are called ducklings. They

eat plants and smaller animals, aka omnivores. The plants are usually in the sea. The smaller

animals are insects, worms, and little fish. Humans usually feed them domesticated bread. I like ducks because:
